Case Study LCA — Harbor Offices

Prepared by: EcoMetrics Studio • DD Snapshot • Reviewer: E. Gomez

Goal & Scope

Quantify embodied carbon (GWP100) for structure & envelope at Design Development Lat per EN 15804+A2. System boundary covers A1–A3, with placeholders for A4/A5. This DD snapshot aims to establish a baseline for mix optimization, procurement planning, and envelope selection aligned with the project’s carbon budget.

Key Inputs

Concrete mixes include 37.04–40.56% SCM; reinforcing steel is EAF with ~95% recycled content; aluminum cladding uses manufacturer EPDs. Assumptions reflect regional supply chains and typical transport distances confirmed with the CM.
